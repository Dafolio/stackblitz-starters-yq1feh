Medical Assstant

As a member of a healthcare team who is responsible for aiding medical professionals in supporting sickly or injured patients, a medical assistant is very similar to a nurse, except they are also responsible for performing administrative tasks within their respective healthcare facilities in order to ensure proper function/order within their facility.

Care Worker

Responsible for the well - being of patients under their care, a care taker should be proficient in providing personal care, and assisting patients with daily activities, and administring medication, and providing emotial support

pharmacist

As a healthcare professional who has expertise in medicine and their usage, they have the responsibility of prescribing medications that are safe and effective to make sure that the patient receives proper care.

physician

As a doctor, a physician is responsible for diagnosing and treating injuries, illnesses, etc. and prescribing medications so that a patient can get safe medication from a pharmacist. They also have teh capability to develop treament plans for any patient sufferent from an illness or an injury.

Mental health counselor

As a mental health counselor, one should have the social ability required to communicate with a patient, and the patience to emphasize with the patient to try an understand their problem/where they are coming from in order to give them advice on how to solve their problem and provide them with some form of emotional comfort or support.
